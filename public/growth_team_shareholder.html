TRIAGE DECISION
TRIVIAL / SIMPLE TASK
  • Execute directly - no approval needed
  • Reply to client confirming completion
  • Target SLA: same business day
  • Log task completion in team tracker
  • No sync needed before executing
Done before end of day
COMPLEX / STRATEGIC TASK
  • Acknowledge client with ETA (within 1h)
  • Align internally - async or quick sync
  • Execute after alignment confirmed
  • Confirm outcome to client post-execution
  • Document decision rationale
Closed with confirmed outcome
ESCALATION / SENSITIVE
  • Flag immediately to Growth Lead (Ryan)
  • Do not respond to client until aligned
  • Loop in Vienesia if budget / relationship risk
  • Confirm approach before any action
  • Document and close with full visibility
Nothing goes out unreviewed
Every request enters triage - no task falls through the gap

.com Domain · Brand Awareness
Objective
Increase .com brand awareness in Indonesia. Nurture audiences on .com usage and drive domain registration consideration.
Challenge
Multiple reporting requirements across performance, webpage & brand safety deliverables. Complex asset mapping for effective campaign setup.
Solution
Optimised reporting framework for consistency and timely delivery. Structured asset mapping aligned creatives with objectives and user journey.

Campaign architecture is critical when managing multiple deliverables. Performance-driven optimisation must align with each reporting layer simultaneously.
MAYO CLINIC
Healthcare · Awareness & Lead Gen
Objective
Increase awareness & consideration of Mayo Clinic in Indonesia. Capture demand across priority treatment categories. Drive qualified lead actions.

Proactively delivered tasks to minimise delays. Structured campaign planning reduced dependencies. Consistent follow-ups maintained progress despite time-zone constraints.

UHNWI healthcare targeting requires precision. Meta drives cost-efficient visibility; Search captures high-intent users via long-tail keywords.

CLIENT HIGHLIGHTS

Wink · Ideoworks Global  ·  Managed by Nahdah · Ads Operations
WINK
Lead Generation · Meta · TikTok · Google
Objective
Generate leads from parents with children aged 3-7 via Meta (Instagram & Facebook), TikTok, and Google (SEM, PMAX & YouTube). Subscription-based kids learning platform.
Challenge
Frequent campaign setup changes disrupted the learning phase, impacting performance stability and CPA efficiency across channels.
Solution
Maintained internal alignment and clear task ownership throughout setup changes. Communicated performance implications to the client proactively. Monitored results post-adjustment and adapted campaigns rapidly to recover.
Insight
CPA volatility was largely driven by setup changes, not core optimisations. Transparent communication aligns strategy with achievable performance outcomes.
IDEOWORKS GLOBAL
B2B Lead Gen · Meta · LinkedIn
Internal
Objective
Generate qualified leads from CEOs, Directors and C-suite decision makers planning ASEAN expansion. Channels: Meta (IG & FB) + LinkedIn Ads. Internal Ideoworks initiative.
Challenge
Target a niche audience - C-suite decision makers planning to expand into Indonesia - and convert them into qualified leads at scale without large budgets.
Solution
Retargeted warm audience + lookalike expansion for scale. Evaluated creative performance and rebuilt best-performing ad styles. Refined messaging to match decision maker's intent signals.
Insight
CEO and Director roles are a niche audience - broad targeting wastes budget. Continuous creative testing is the highest-leverage optimisation lever in this segment.
